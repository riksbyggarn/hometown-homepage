Visit Gothenburg

Discover the charm of Sweden’s west coast – vibrant culture, stunning archipelago, and friendly locals await you in Gothenburg.

Top three activities to do in Gothenburg

Experience Frölunda HC Live

Experience the excitement and atmosphere as Frölunda HC takes the ice at Scandinavium – a must for all sports fans!

Watch IFK Göteborg Play

Experience the passion of Swedish football as IFK Göteborg takes the field at Gamla Ullevi – a thrilling event for all fans!

Visit Liseberg Amusement Park

Enjoy thrilling rides, fun games, and beautiful gardens at Liseberg – Scandinavia’s most popular amusement park, perfect for all ages!
